However, the underlying risk assumed is also higher in terms of capital loss. Still, this opportunity cost cannot be ignored by investors.</p>\n<h2 id=\"6h00s\"><strong>Who Should Consider Fixed Deposits?</strong></h2>\n<p><strong>Income-Seeking Investors Prioritizing Capital Preservation</strong></p>\n<p>For conservative investors like retirees and pensioners prioritizing capital protection and wealth preservation over high growth and risk, fixed deposits make for ideal vehicles delivering guaranteed regular payouts in the form of interest income.</p>\n<p><strong>Individuals with Short-Term Savings Goals</strong></p>\n<p>FDs serve well for those with financial goals maturing over the next 1-3 years like vacations, automobile purchases or accumulating funds for home down payments.
